# Hero image — prompt (Strategy Partners)

Paleta: ver `brand-palette.css`.

## Prompt principal (foto real + texto na imagem)

Use em Midjourney, DALL·E, Ideogram, Flux, etc.

**Cenário recomendado (escolha 1 no início do prompt):** vista aérea do litoral / mar ao entardecer · sala de reunião executiva com janela para cidade · escritório alto padrão em São Paulo com luz natural.

```
Premium corporate advisory hero banner for a Brazilian strategy consultancy, photorealistic wide 3:2 composition.

BACKGROUND — real photography, not illustration:
Choose one authentic scene with depth and horizon:
(A) Aerial or elevated view of coastline and ocean at blue hour — calm sea, subtle waves, distant shore, mood of scale and long-term vision; OR
(B) Executive boardroom with floor-to-ceiling glass overlooking a modern business district at dusk — empty chairs, polished table, no people; OR
(C) High-floor office interior looking out to sky and city — natural light, minimal furniture silhouette, sense of decisions made at altitude.

Apply a cinematic color grade: deep navy overlay #192D55 at 55–70% opacity on the left two-thirds so white text stays readable; let the real photo breathe on the right third (sea horizon, city lights, or window light). Subtle film grain, professional editorial photo, shot on full-frame camera, 35mm lens, f/2.8, natural colors shifted toward cool blues with warm gold accents only in UI elements.

Typography embedded in the image, left-aligned on the darkened overlay zone:

- Small label: "§ 01 — POSICIONAMENTO" in white at 70% opacity, refined sans-serif.

- Pretitle in white #FFFFFF, clean sans-serif:
  "Uma Consultoria Agêntica independente especializada em resolver
  situações complexas de seu negócio."

- Main headline in large white serif:
  "As decisões mais caras da sua empresa raramente
  aparecem isoladas no orçamento."
  Only the word "raramente" in metallic gold #E6A023.

- Buttons (flat UI):
  Primary: gold #E6A023 fill, navy #192D55 text "Apresentar uma decisão".
  Secondary: transparent, 1px white border, white text "Ver cases recentes".

Mood: trustworthy, institutional, horizon and depth — strategy, governance, M&A, restructuring. Brazilian corporate context without clichés. No visible faces, no handshake, no stock-photo smiles. Portuguese spelled exactly as written. Ultra sharp typography, print quality.
```

**Negative prompt:**
```
illustration, CGI, cartoon, terracotta palette, orange background, cream paper, cluttered collage, handshake close-up, diverse team high-five, cryptocurrency, neon, lens flare overload, misspelled Portuguese, English headline, watermark, logo mockup, tropical vacation beach party, palm trees postcard, drone selfie, oversaturated sunset Instagram filter
```

## Variantes só de fundo (foto real, sem texto)

Para sobrepor HTML/CSS depois. Mesma proporção 3:2.

**Mar / horizonte (recomendado para Strategy Partners — visão, escala, decisão):**
```
Photorealistic wide hero background 3:2, elevated view of calm ocean and coastline at blue hour, distant city shoreline optional, deep moody sky, cinematic color grade with navy #192D55 gradient overlay from left (65% width) to transparent right, subtle film grain, empty left area for text, no people, no boats close-up, no text, no logos, executive editorial photography, full-frame quality
```

**Ambiente de trabalho / decisão:**
```
Photorealistic wide hero background 3:2, empty executive boardroom with glass wall overlooking São Paulo skyline at dusk, polished conference table, cool natural light, navy #192D55 gradient overlay on left two-thirds for text legibility, no people, no text, institutional financial advisory mood, editorial corporate photography
```

**Terra / infraestrutura (setores regulados, energia, capital intensivo):**
```
Photorealistic wide hero background 3:2, aerial view of industrial coastline or port infrastructure at distance, soft atmospheric haze, serious tone not dystopian, navy overlay left side #192D55, gold accent as thin light reflection on water optional #E6A023, no people, no text, premium strategy consulting aesthetic
```
